In honor of Joan Feynman: Women in astrophysics

Mon, January 12

Triple Peaks Library Conference Hall & Online

At this event, which celebrates the library's namesake, our guest lecturer will take us on a journey exploring some of the most prominent female researchers in the world of space science. It's easy to buy into the misconception that this field was mostly dominated by men, but our lecture aims to change that notion and prove that this hasn't been the case since at least the 18th century.

That our readers love

  • The Golden Lily

    The Bloodlines series by Richelle Mead is a spin-off of the Vampire Academy universe. Following Sydney Sage, an alchemist protecting vampire secrets, it delves into her struggle with duty and her romance with the charismatic vampire Adrian Ivashkov. Filled with intrigue and supernatural elements, the series offers a captivating exploration of love and loyalty in a complex paranormal world.

    Richelle Mead

  • Twilight

    The Twilight series by Stephenie Meyer follows Bella Swan's forbidden love with Edward Cullen, a vampire, in the rainy town of Forks, Washington. Across four books—Twilight, New Moon, Eclipse, and Breaking Dawn—readers are drawn into a world of romance, supernatural beings, and high school drama, exploring themes of love, sacrifice, and immortality.

    Stephenie Meyer

  • Lorien Legacies

    The Lorien Legacies series by Pittacus Lore follows nine alien teenagers, the Garde, fleeing to Earth after their planet, Lorien, is invaded by the villainous Mogadorians. Hunted down one by one, they must harness their powers and unite to defeat their enemies. Filled with action and adventure, the series captivates readers with its thrilling narrative and diverse characters.

    Pittacus Lore

  • Throne of Glass

    The Throne of Glass series by Sarah J. Maas tracks the odyssey of Celaena Sardothien, a lethal assassin thrust into a world of political intrigue and magic. Across several books, she uncovers her destiny as a formidable force against evil. With richly crafted characters and gripping storytelling, the series enthralls readers with its blend of fantasy and romance.

    Sarah J. Mass

About the library

Libraries are essential establishments in practically every city, and the Triple Peaks Library is no exception. It first opened its doors way back in 2018, right as our humble town began bidding welcome to its very first citizens!

Since its initial opening, the library has seen nearly 342 monthly visitors. That's almost 50% of our population! So far, most citizens have enjoyed reading books and using our ample online resources.

Become a member today, and make sure to check out our Staff Picks section. This week's selection is full of books anyone interested in computer programming would love!
